# Best CRM for Shopify: A Data-Quality-First Comparison (2026)

This guide approaches Shopify CRM selection from the data layer up, not the feature list down.

## The problem with most Shopify CRM guides

Every comparison starts at the CRM selection stage. This one starts one step earlier: what does your Shopify customer data look like before it reaches any CRM?

Key findings from 2026 research:

- 20 to 30% of ecommerce revenue is never recorded due to ad blockers, ITP, and connection issues (Littledata 2026)
- Shopify customer exports typically contain 15 to 35% duplicate records
- Shopify does not include GDPR consent status in default customer exports
- Attribution gap between Shopify (last-click) and Meta (7-day click / 1-day view) is 15 to 30% of reported revenue
